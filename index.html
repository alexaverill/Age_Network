Who We Are
We are a registered young feminine non-profit organization with objectives focusing on African women: ending child marriage, advance gender equality in education, promote women's good health, political, leadership and economic inclusion for a fairer future.
Vision
To become Africa's No. 1 movement for gender equality, young women and girls' capacity development, leadership and empowerment hub, a platform through which women become extricable partners in the sustainable development.
To empower, support, and protect women and girls, to advance their bodily rights, leadership, economic and financial inclusion in Nigeria and to foster them to become extricable partners in the sustainable development of the society for our generation and most importantly the next generations.
